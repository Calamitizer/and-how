Crawler

A Python CLI web-scraper that bundles the serial fictions of Wildbow (primarily Worm) locally, for offline reading. As a web serial, Worm was published in over 300 installments, each on a separate webpage, which makes for nail-biting serial reading and laborious binge-reading.
